Short bio

Thiago Tarraf Varella studies computational psychology and neuroscience. He is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Psychology at Princeton University. He currently works in the Developmental Neuromechanics & Communication Lab at Princeton University with Professor Asif A. Ghazanfar. He previously worked in the Timing and Cognition Lab at Universidade Federal do ABC with Professor Marcelo B. Reyes and Professor Raphael Y. de Camargo.

Thiago completed his undergraduation sutdying mathematics and neuroscience in the Molecular Sciences Program at Universidade de São Paulo in August 2018.
