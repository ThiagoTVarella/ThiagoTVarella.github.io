Scheduler

Hi! Thanks for maybe using this scheduler. The current intended use of this website is to provide a schedule for interviews between students and professors, given that students have preferred professors to have the interview with, and professors have specific time availabilities. The timeslots are enumerated from 1 to the number of timeslots.

If you already have all the data of the preferences for each student and availability for each professor, click here to enter that data manually.

If you haven't yet conducted the survey of the student preferred interviewers and professor's availability, click here for instructions on how to proceed.
