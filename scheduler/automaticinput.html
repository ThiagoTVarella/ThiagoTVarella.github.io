Import data

Save the google sheets from the forms as a .csv file. To do that, you:

  1. Open the google forms
  2. Click on "Responses"
  3. Click on "View in Sheets"
  4. Click on "File"
  5. Click on "Download"
  6. Click on "Comma-separated values (.csv)"
